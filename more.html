About Us

Black Umbrella is a safety net made up of a collective of activists and grassroot organizers in America uniting, sharing resources, and supporting one another.

We are a deliberative community body focused on educating, organizing and acting to dismantle institutionalized racism and discrimination. Our goal is to elevate consciousness to obtain Black liberation on a global spectrum.

We aim to rebuild America so that it reflects our collective moral values. We have a vision of a perfect union where we all have equal opportunities at liberty, justice and the pursuit of happiness.
